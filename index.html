About Us

IMMINENT’s mission is to assist great companies & brands to attract, retain and inspire their most valuable resource –Employees.

Ours is a holistic approach to workforce wellbeing that addresses emotional, physical, workplace environment and social wellness. We call it Employee360™.

Our technology empowers employees to become corporate stakeholders leading to greater health and productivity. We believe presenting charitable opportunities is not only a corporate social responsibility but a compassionate path to emotional self-esteem. Our tools encourage employees create compelling storytelling about their volunteerism that build authentic relationships with each other and the public.

We accomplish all of this because great brands understand the concept of inside-out marketing where their first customer is within. And when that customer-employee is happy, they share that happiness with the world.
